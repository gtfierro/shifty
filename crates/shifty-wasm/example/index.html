Ontology workbench

Uploaded shape ontologies and fetched imports selected for validation and inference.

How this works
  • Uploading or pasting a shapes graph parses its owl:imports and fetches them automatically (http URLs are normalized to https).
  • Each import shows a status: ready, fetching, missing (couldn't be resolved automatically — upload it manually), or error.
  • The checkbox controls whether that ontology is merged into the shapes graph used for validation/inference — unchecked ontologies are excluded even if fetched.
  • Selections persist across reloads (stored in your browser's local storage).
